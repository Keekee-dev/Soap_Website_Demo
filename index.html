FAQs

Questions

Example questions for now
Are the soaps all natural? +
Yes. Every bar is made with natural oils butters and botanicals, scented only with essential oils and never artificial fragrance.
How long does a bar last? +
Kept on a dry dish between washes a bar lasts several weeks. Letting it dry out fully is the trick to making it last.
Do you ship or is it pickup only? +
Local pickup around Victoria is easy to arrange, and shipping across the island and beyond can be sorted too. Just get in touch.
Can I order a custom batch? +
Yes. Custom bars for weddings gifts and events are some of my favourite work. Reach out with what you have in mind.
Where can I buy in person? +
Catch me at markets around Victoria through the season. The visit section below has the where and the when.
